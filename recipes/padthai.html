Pad Thai

Description

A classic dish from Thailand

My personal favourite

Ingredients

    300 g diced chicken / prawns (remove black thing in middle)
    200 g flat rice noodles (soak in warm water for 10 min)
    2tbsp crushed peanuts
    200 g tofu cubes (optional)
    half a red onion
    2 tbsp fish sauce
    3 tbsp sugar
    1 tbsp sweet radish / tamarind paste
    2 eggs
    3 spring onions
    handful of bean sprouts
    1 lime
    a little bit of chili powder

Steps

    Cook tofu first in oil
    Add onion and meat
    Cook rice noodles for 1-2 min
    Add 2 tbsp fish sauce
    Add 3 tbsp sugar
    Add 1 tbsp sweet radish (diced) / or tamarind paste
    Use water for cooking
    Check noodles with spatula handle end - if sticky cook longer
    Cook 2 eggs next to the pad thai
    Mix together when eggs are ready
    Add 3 spring onions (sliced)
    1 handful of bean sprouts
    Serve up, and add 1 lime, peanuts, sugar and chili powder if needed
