Brownies

Description

My favourite brownie recipe.

A classic from Canada.

Ingredients

    1 1/2 C flour
    1 1/2 tps salt
    2 C sugar
    1 C oil (a little bit less than 1 C)
    4 eggs
    2 tps vanilla (essence?)
    7 tbsp Cocoa
    2 tbsp soft butter (not melted) 5 sec, then 10 sec on high
    Optional: Cut 1 slab of white chocolate and add it.

Steps

    Mix oil & eggs together, mix with fork.
    Add rest and mix again.
    Put in oven for 180 / 25min then just under 5min / 3min 30 sec
